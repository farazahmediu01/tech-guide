---

## Slide 3 — The 5-Layer Cake of AI (Jensen Huang, NVIDIA)

Modern AI is built like a five-layer cake — from the bottom up:

| Layer | What It Is |
|-------|-----------|
| 1. Energy | Powers the data centers |
| 2. Chips | GPUs doing trillions of calculations |
| 3. Infrastructure | Cloud platforms & supercomputers |
| 4. Models | Neural networks that reason & generate |
| **5. Applications** | **Where AI becomes *useful*** |

> Billions are invested in layers 1–4 so that **Layer 5 can exist.**

**This book is entirely about Layer 5** — building the applications, agents, and digital workers that turn AI capability into real value.

---

## Slide 4 — The Core Idea: What is a Digital FTE?

**FTE** = Full-Time Equivalent (one full-time human employee's work capacity)

**Digital FTE** = The AI equivalent — an intelligent agent that:

- Performs structured knowledge work
- Operates **continuously** (no breaks, no sleep)
- Scales **instantly** (deploy 1 or 1,000)
- Works within **human oversight**

> "A Digital FTE is not just a model with a prompt. It is a **system**."

---

## Slide 5 — The 4 Elements That Make a Digital FTE Reliable

A Digital FTE is built from **four critical elements**:

| Element | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Structured Specifications** | Defines exactly what the agent must do |
| **Domain Expertise** | The knowledge engine guiding its reasoning |
| **Engineering Architecture** | Ensures reliability & scalability |
| **Human Oversight** | Maintains accountability & governance |

> Without all four, you have a prototype. With all four, you have a **scalable asset**.

## Slide 9 — The Solution: The Agent Factory Method

This book teaches a **repeatable system** to:

1. **Identify** valuable AI employee opportunities
2. **Encode** expert knowledge into structured specifications
3. **Design** bounded, reliable agent workflows
4. **Deploy** on cloud-native infrastructure (Docker, Kubernetes)
5. **Govern** with human-in-the-loop oversight

> The goal: Build an **Agent Factory** — a repeatable capability for producing Digital FTEs again and again.
